Minecraft Speedrunning

One of my hobbies is Minecraft speedrunning, where I go by the username tssal. This started out as a low-key fun activity when I was stuck at home in 2020 but eventually turned into something that I became more competitive about. I upload my runs to YouTube (tssal_game) and occasionally stream on Twitch (tssal31).

I mainly run 1.16 Random Seed Glitchless (the most popular category) and Half% (get half, or 40, of all of the advancements). I also occasionally play other categories, which can be found on my channel. I also sometimes compete in MCSR Ranked, a 1v1 speedrun race system.

Update Spring 2025: I don't play this nearly as much as I used to, so many stats and rankings are very low compared to what they used to be.

NBA Playoffs Bracket Challenge

Every year, I run a fun bracket challenge among my friends, with a unique scoring formula that incorporates predicted series winners, number of games for series, and statistical leaders. Currently, the bracket challenge is run in a small-scale operation, but I have plans to make the process a bit more online in the near future.
