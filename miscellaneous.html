Minecraft Speedrunning

One of my hobbies is Minecraft speedrunning, where I go by the username tssal. This started out as a low-key fun activity when I was stuck at home in 2020 but eventually turned into something that I became more competitive about. I upload my runs to YouTube (tssal_game) and occasionally stream on Twitch (tssal31).

I mainly compete in MCSR Ranked, a 1v1 speedrun race system. Besides that, I also run 1.16 Random Seed Glitchless (the most popular leaderboard timing category). I also occasionally play other categories such as Half% (get half, or 40, of all of the advancements) and other versions (1.15).

NBA Playoffs Bracket Challenge

Every year since 2017, I run a fun bracket challenge among my friends, with a unique scoring formula that incorporates predicted series winners, number of games for series, and statistical leaders. Currently, the bracket challenge is run in a small-scale operation, but I have plans to make the process a bit more online in the near future.
