About Me

Hi! I'm Tristan Shin. I'm currently a PhD student at UC San Diego. I did my undergrad at MIT in music (course 21M) and math (course 18) with a minor in computer science. After that, I did my master's in math at the University of Cambridge (Part III). I am passionate about math and music, especially about sharing what I can with others. I have served as the music director of the MIT IAP Orchestra, the tournament director of HMMT, and have been involved in various other orchestras and math programs.

To see more about what I work on, check out the tabs in the header.
