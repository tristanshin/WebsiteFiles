About Me

Hi! I'm Tristan Shin. I'm currently a PhD student in Mathematics at UC San Diego. I did my undergrad at MIT in music (course 21M) and math (course 18) with a minor in computer science. After that, I did my master's in math at the University of Cambridge (Part III). I am passionate about math and music, especially about sharing what I can with others. I currently write problems for the American Regions Mathematics League, teach and coach math at the San Diego Math Circle, and play trumpet with the La Jolla Symphony Orchestra and the UCSD Chamber Orchestra. I have served as the music director of the MIT IAP Orchestra, the tournament director of HMMT, and have been involved in various other orchestras and math programs.
